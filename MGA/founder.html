Founder

Hello, I am Dhananjay Pandey, the founder of Mission Green Ayodhya.

With a vision of making Ayodhya clean and green, I started this initiative two years ago. Over this time, our NGO has grown to include 1,000 active volunteers who have planted more than 1,500 plants in and around Ayodhya.

Our mission is simple: to create a cleaner and greener Ayodhya for future generations. Join us in our efforts to make this vision a reality!

Feel free to reach out to us to be a part of this wonderful journey.
