About Mission Green Ayodhya Foundation

Mission Green Ayodhya Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) founded by passionate young leaders and prominent personalities from Ayodhya. This initiative is deeply rooted in the vision of preserving and enhancing the natural beauty and environment of Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Ayodhya.

The foundation aims to transform Ayodhya into India's cleanest and greenest city by addressing critical environmental challenges such as deforestation and pollution. It actively works to:

  • Prevent Tree Cutting: Raising awareness and implementing measures to curb deforestation in the region.
  • Promote Large-Scale Tree Plantation: Organizing massive plantation drives to enhance greenery and improve air quality.

Core Belief

The foundation believes it is everyone's moral duty to plant a tree and take responsibility for its growth. By doing so, we can collectively ensure fresh oxygen levels for future generations and foster a sustainable environment.

Vision

Mission Green Ayodhya envisions Ayodhya as a beacon of environmental sustainability, blending its cultural heritage with ecological consciousness. Through community-driven efforts, the foundation strives to make Ayodhya a model city that inspires similar initiatives across India.

By joining hands with Mission Green Ayodhya, individuals and communities can contribute to preserving the sanctity and environment of this historic city.
